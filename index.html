FAR CASPIAN

DISCOGRAPHY

-

Far Caspian is an indie bedroom pop band whose music blends melancholy and euphoria, reminiscent of wandering through a dream. Their nostalgic melodies and ethereal vocals create a fulfilling journey through introspection and emotion. With lush instrumentation and evocative lyrics, Far Caspian invites listeners to escape into their unique sonic landscape, leaving an indelible mark on all who experience their music.
